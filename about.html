The Founder Journey

Artisans Of Light & Glass

Founded in 2012 by master glass technician Marcus Vance, Aura 3D started with a singular vision: to replace generic flat-printed merchandise with stunning, high-fidelity physical sculptures.

Combining Vance's knowledge of optical refractivity and German laser diode technology, we pioneered subsurface marking pipelines that achieve microscopic depth gradients without compromising outer structural polish.

Uncompromising Standards

K9 Optical Glass & Precision Green Diodes

The foundation of every Aura 3D piece is premium-grade K9 borosilicate glass. Renowned for its extreme durability, high refractivity, and absolute bubble-free clarity, our crystal is cut and hand-polished by master lapidaries.

Inside this flawless medium, our state-of-the-art German-engineered green laser diodes (operating at a precise 532nm wavelength) focus deep beneath the polished surface. Without causing even a micro-fracture on the exterior, they create thousands of microscopic points of high-definition light scatter that form the 3D sculpture inside.
